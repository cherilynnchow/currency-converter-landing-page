Currency Converter

Get Started

Open-source MIT Licensed.

Currency Converter is a Chrome Extension that seamlessly integrates real-time currency conversion into the user's browsing experience. With the click of a button, users can instantly convert prices on any webpage to their home currency or a preferred currency. It eliminates the need to disrupt the browsing experience with manual calculations or external websites, offering a streamlined, in-page conversion feature. Users also have the ability to manually input numbers and convert between currencies if they wish to.
